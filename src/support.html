Troubleshooting

These pointers refer to a faulty VPN connection, which significantly slows down your download speed.

Download premium VPN

The best way to prevent unwanted errors is to install a proven, reliable VPN. Premium VPNs are always the best choice as they are reliable, have sophisticated systems and provide access to dedicated customer service teams.

Generally, quality VPNs such as NordVPN, ExpressVPN, CyberGhost VPN, and SurfShark VPN are an investment. Each of these services has the highest characteristics and an excellent reputation.

By choosing one, you will reduce the likelihood of your provider crashing or causing problems.


Server change

Changing your server is one of the easiest fixes for a slow or unreliable VPN connection. Some servers are faster than others and this can be due to many reasons.

Typically, servers located in physically closer locations can significantly affect connection speed. Certain servers may also become overcrowded with users, slowing down performance.

Luckily, most VPN services make it easy to switch servers with extensive networks. Some even have a feature that automatically connects to the best available server.


Changing the port

The network port can play an important role in the speed of your connection. If you are experiencing some difficulties, we recommend that you try switching ports.

Changing ports can improve your connection, as sometimes ISPs can restrict certain numbers. Sometimes bad connections can be caused by some ports being faster than others.
