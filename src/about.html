About CorpVPN

We at CorpVPN strongly believe that the internet could be more transparent, private, and secure. That’s why our engineers, system administrators, and designers have spent the last 10 years developing a VPN service powered by next-generation encryption to bring this vision closer to reality.

Security

Without a VPN, your sensitive data is vulnerable to hackers. This is especially the case with public Wi-Fi networks in cafes, hotels, and airports: Allowing unrestricted access to anyone with a laptop, these networks are often monitored by hackers trying to steal data.

How VPN
encryption works

VPNs use public-key encryption to protect the transfer of AES keys. The server uses the public key of the VPN client to encrypt the key and then sends it to the client. The client program on your computer than decrypts that message using its own private key.
